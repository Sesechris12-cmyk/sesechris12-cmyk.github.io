Aroma Coffee shop

This project involves creating an interactive sales analytics dashboard for Aroma Coffee Shop to monitor and analyze weekly sales performance across multiple locations in Indonesia. The dashboard provides real-time insights into key business metrics, enabling data-driven decision-making for operational efficiency and revenue optimization.The analysis focuses on several critical areas, including sales trends, product performance, inventory management, location-based sales distribution, and order tracking. By visualizing these metrics through an interactive Tableau dashboard, the project aims to help the coffee shop identify top-performing products, optimize stock levels, understand geographic sales patterns, and improve overall business performance. The dashboard serves as a comprehensive tool for store managers and business owners to track weekly KPIs, compare performance against previous periods, and make informed decisions that drive customer satisfaction and profitability.

E-Commerce Business Insight

This project involves performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to analyze customer behavior, purchase patterns, and operational metrics for Prism Commerce, a multi-category e-commerce retailer. Through comprehensive data visualization and analysis, the dashboard provides actionable insights to optimize business strategy, enhance customer satisfaction, and drive revenue growth. The analysis examines critical business dimensions, including customer demographics, spending patterns, pricing sensitivity, satisfaction impact, shipping preferences, and product performance. By leveraging interactive filters and dynamic visualizations, stakeholders can explore data across multiple segments to inform strategic decision-making.
